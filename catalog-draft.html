15–16 — Accessories

Accessories &
System Extensions

Every Perfostand display system is designed to work with our full accessories range. Accessories extend the system, increase display density, and allow the same base unit to serve multiple categories.

All accessories are manufactured in-house to the same standard as the main units — same steel, same finish, same tolerances.

[ IMAGE — accessories flatlay or exploded view ]

System Philosophy

Buy the frame once. Configure it for any category with accessories. Change the hook pitch, add bins, swap printed panels — no new frame needed. This is the Perfostand advantage.

Hooks

Single, double, and looped. Multiple pitch options. Chrome and powder coat finish.

Dividers

Adjustable shelf dividers for product separation and category organisation.

Bins & Trays

Open-front bins for small products, fixings, and consumables.

Wheels & Bases

Lockable castors for mobile units. Levelling feet for static installations.

Lighting

LED shelf lighting strips. Header lightbox units. Wiring concealed in frame.

Printed Panels

Drop-in graphic panels for seasonal or campaign updates.

Acrylic Holders

Price tag strips, leaflet holders, info pockets in clear and branded acrylic.

Shelf Stoppers

Front-facing shelf guards to prevent product fall at edge of shelf.

Full accessories catalogue available separately. Custom accessories manufactured to order. MOQ applies.
