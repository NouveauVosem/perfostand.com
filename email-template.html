My name is Valeriy Valerievich, and I represent Alvla s.r.o. For more than 30 years, we have been developing and manufacturing branded retail shelving and display solutions for tool manufacturers and distributors.

Among our long-term clients are King Tony (France), Richmann Sp. z o.o. (Poland), Altuna HNOS (Spain), as well as official distributors of BOSCH, Makita, and Motul in Ukraine. This confirms that our products meet European market requirements and quality standards.

Our shelving systems are fully customized according to the client's requirements, including dimensions, branding, product assortment, and display format. We manufacture solutions with various types of hooks, shelves, toppers, and additional elements depending on the products being presented.

One of the key advantages of our systems is their knock-down construction, which allows the entire display to fit into packaging corresponding to the dimensions of a standard 800×1200 mm pallet. Compared to traditional welded constructions, this significantly simplifies warehousing, transportation, and distribution to retail locations.

We offer:

manufacturing based on individual technical specifications
flexible order volumes, including small batches starting from 40 display units
branded solutions in various color combinations
competitive pricing under current market conditions
consistent quality control at every stage of production
support from our in-house designers and engineers

We would be glad to discuss your requirements and prepare an individual proposal. Please feel free to reach out — we are happy to answer any questions.
